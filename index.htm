Window Washing - Why Choose Camelot

Reduce Allergens

Window washing helps keep your home healthy! On average almost 70% of all dust and pollen come through your windows. The window screens act as a partial filter for this dust and pollen. When cleaned professionally they can greatly reduce the amount of dust and allergens blown in.
